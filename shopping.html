Shop Responsibly

Waste managed at source becomes a resource. To close the loop in a circular economy, Circle Up by Saahas Zero Waste creates products made from recycled and up-cycled waste materials. The waste materials are segregated based on reusability, washed, dried and then transformed into functional products.By choosing these alternative solutions, we can reduce the demand for consumption of virgin resources, thereby reducing the burden on the environment. These products are integrated into our Social Inclusion programme for which we work with various Self- Help Groups, including women/men from difficult economic backgrounds and differently-abled persons.
